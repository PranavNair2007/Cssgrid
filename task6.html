Task Question and Answer

What are grid lines?

They are the vertical and horizontal lines between columns and rows.

How many columns does the item span?

It spans two columns from line 1 to 3.

What happens to other items?

Other items move to next available space in the grid.

Can two items overlap?

Yes if they placed on same grid lines.

Where is column spanning used in websites?

Used for headers, section, footer, etc.
